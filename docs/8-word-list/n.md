## n


## N/A

Abbreviation for *not available* or *not applicable*. Spell out on first reference.

Not *NA, N-A,* or *na*.

## name server

Two words. Not *nameserver* or *name-server*.

## namespace

One word. Not *name space* or *name-space*.
